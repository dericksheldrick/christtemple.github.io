About Us

We aim to improve lives by empowering our community spiritually, economically, and behaviorally according to God's Word.

Statement Of Faith

We Believe... there is One God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

We Believe... in the deity of Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, and His resurrection.

We Believe... the Bible is the inspired Word of God.

Vision

Matthew 28:19-20, Acts 2:42-47

We are committed to developing and equipping God's people to grow into maturity in Christ, and to improve living standards in the community.

Mission

  • Influencing Society through Christ
  • Raising Leaders
  • Shaping Vision

Through our programs, we aim to make the lives of our community better and empower them spiritually and economically according to God's Word.
